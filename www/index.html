  • The Mission:

    Fathom, is a company that produces 3D sensing technology. Our mission is to create and broaden this technology to modern applications and applied systems.

    The three pillars of Fathom are: Interact, Discover, and Experience. Each of these pillars creates the foundation of Fathom, representing our core values.

    We generate interaction by providing the tools necessary to start developing 3D sensing technology. When developers interact with Fathom, they become the architects of interaction.

    We crave discovery when developing technology. New ideas and interactions drive our architects to build something amazing.

    We value experience through application. When our architects develop a new experience, the impact it creates is evident and thoughtful.

    We are developing a new dimension when interacting with technology. Comprehension and application of our three pillars is what makes Fathom’s foundation strong.

  • We Generate Interaction

    Fathom has created a new form of interaction. 3D sensing technology needs to be explored and Fathom is up to that challange. We will push the limits of digital interaction for established technologies.

  • We Crave Discovery

    Fathom wants technology to be explored. Fathom has created an open-source database and forum for 3D sensing technology. If you have an idea of how this technology can improve lives, and have the passion to see that through, we want to hear about it.

  • We Value Experiences

    Fathom has a passion for creating new forms of human-computer interaction. We create experiences unlike any other. Our ideas, designs, and applications will change the world. We want you to be a part of that change.
